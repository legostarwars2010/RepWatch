Privacy Policy

RepWatch is built on a simple principle: your privacy matters.

What We Don't Collect

We don't collect personal information. We don't track you. We don't sell data. We don't use cookies for advertising or analytics.

When you search by address, the lookup happens in your browser and is sent to our server only to find your district. We don't store your address.

What We Do Collect

Basic server logs (IP addresses, timestamps) are collected automatically for security purposes. These logs are not used for tracking.

Third-Party Services

Neon (Database)

Stores public voting records. No personal user data is stored.

U.S. Census TIGERweb

Resolves addresses to districts. Your address is sent temporarily for lookup only.

OpenAI

Generates bill summaries. No user data is sent to OpenAI.

No Accounts Required

RepWatch doesn't require you to create an account or log in. All data is publicly accessible.
